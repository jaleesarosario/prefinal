et Moi

Jaleesa Rosario is an Icelandic Born Afro Caribbean American modern dancer and mixed media motion artist, who has found a creative passion for...UX research and HCI agile design with a focus on information architecture, complex systems and computational linguistics.

Her research is dedicated to the creative (re)organization of HCI systems and AI training methods, in order to cultivate more inclusive NLP environments.

As an Interaction Designer, Jaleesa has leveraged her passion for creative design thinking to support her career in technology, studying at the University of Michigan at the School of Information.

Some of her highlights include a feature in Amy Poehlers Smart Girls, live video production for GooglePixel, and the IT development designer for the Brooklyn Museum's Library & Archives... just to name a few.
